Image Sizing Example

Sizing Images while maintaining the aspect ratio

Below shows an example of how to take an image and maintain its aspect ratio within a given container. For this example, we will show the image in its actual size on the left and on the right show the image in a smaller container which is constrained both in height and in width. The image in the container will maintain its aspect ratio.

The radio buttons below simply change the class on the image container and the image on the right so that the image is sized (small, deium or large) and maintains its aspect ratio while centering it both horizontally and vertically inside the image container.
